History

Back in 2015, students of one class had a project to be submitted before the end of the term. They were grouped and the maximum members per group are 3 members each. Then came 3 students who gathered together at a certain corner of that classroom and formed a group. Two friends, Kevin and Billy were seated at each other, not knowing who to group with. Then Ace came in, who also happened to not have a group as well. Eventually, Kevin and Billy formed a group with Ace and they started planning of what project they would make. After a series of brainstorming and discussions, they decided to go for a car business.
